About ReadyNow

ReadyNow is an AI Disaster Readiness Assistant designed to help users prepare for disasters by providing decision-support guidance and evacuation information.

Disclaimer

ReadyNow is a decision-support tool only. It does not replace official emergency services or government advisories. Users should always follow instructions from local authorities.

Data Sources

  • PAGASA (Philippine Atmospheric, Geophysical and Astronomical Services Administration) – Source of official weather information and advisories.
  • NDRRMC (National Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Council) – Reference for disaster preparedness guidelines and safety protocols.
  • Local Government Units (LGUs) – Basis for evacuation centers and barangay-level advisories.

Evacuation center data displayed in this application are sample representations used for academic and demonstration purposes only.
